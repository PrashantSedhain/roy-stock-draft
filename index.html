The Fine Print

How the score is kept

Each holding begins at 6.67% of its participant’s portfolio. Long return is current price divided by the July 17, 2026 close, minus one. Short return reverses that calculation. The portfolio result is the arithmetic mean of all fifteen position returns.

Market timing

Quotes refresh during regular US market hours and retain the most recent close outside those hours. Since July 17, 2027 falls on Saturday, final tradable prices are captured at market close Friday, July 16.
